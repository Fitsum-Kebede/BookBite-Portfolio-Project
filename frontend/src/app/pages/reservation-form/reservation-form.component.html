About Us

Our Story

Founded with a vision to redefine dining in Addis Ababa, BookBite is the brainchild of a team of food enthusiasts, dedicated to celebrating the diverse flavors of Ethiopian cuisine while offering unmatched convenience and hospitality. Drawing inspiration from the bustling streets of Addis Ababa and the warmth of Ethiopian hospitality, we set out to create a space where friends and families could gather to savor delicious meals and create lasting memories.

At BookBite, we take pride in showcasing the best of Ethiopian culinary traditions, with a contemporary twist. From traditional injera platters to modern interpretations of classic dishes, our menu is a celebration of the diverse flavors and textures that make Ethiopian cuisine so unique. Using only the finest locally sourced ingredients and time-honored cooking techniques, our talented chefs craft each dish with care, ensuring a culinary experience that delights the senses and nourishes the soul.

We understand that your time is precious, which is why we've implemented a state-of-the-art reservation system to make dining at BookBite as seamless as possible. With just a few clicks, you can reserve a table for your desired date and time, ensuring that your dining experience is tailored to your preferences. Whether you're planning a romantic dinner for two or a gathering with friends, our reservation system puts you in control, allowing you to focus on what truly matters enjoying great food and great company.

Why Choose BookBite?

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, enjoying a casual meal with friends, or simply craving delicious Ethiopian cuisine, BookBite offers the perfect setting for every occasion. Our cozy yet elegant ambiance provides the ideal backdrop for memorable dining experiences, making us the go-to destination for locals and visitors alike.

Chefs

Our Proffesional Chefs

Selamawit Tadesse

Master Chef

With a passion for preserving and innovating Ethiopian cuisine, Chef Selamawit "Selam" Tadesse brings a wealth of culinary expertise to BookBite. Born and raised in Addis Ababa, Chef Selam developed a deep appreciation for the flavors and traditions of Ethiopian cuisine from a young age.

Kaleb Beyene

Patissier

A master of the grill and a true culinary artist, Chef Kaleb Beyene brings his passion for bold flavors and meticulous craftsmanship to the kitchen at BookBite. With over a decade of experience in the restaurant industry, Chef Kaleb has earned a reputation for his expertly grilled meats and inventive flavor combinations.

William Anderson

Cook

With a background in pastry arts and a flair for creativity, Chef Mahlet Assefa brings a touch of sweetness to the savory delights of Ethiopian cuisine at BookBite. Trained in both traditional and modern pastry techniques, Chef Mahlet's desserts are a celebration of flavor, texture, and artistry.
